About Us

Our mission is to provide a simple and effective tool for detecting pneumonia in chest X-ray images. Pneumonia is a severe lung infection that can have life-threatening consequences if not detected and treated early. Our product leverages advanced machine learning techniques to assist healthcare professionals and improve the accuracy and speed of diagnosis.

Our Motivation

The motivation behind our product is to make pneumonia detection accessible and affordable to everyone. We aim to bridge the gap in healthcare by providing a tool that can be used in various healthcare settings, from well-equipped hospitals to remote clinics. Early detection of pneumonia can save lives, and we believe that technology can play a crucial role in achieving this goal.

Key Features

  • Fast and accurate pneumonia detection
  • User-friendly interface for easy image upload
  • Access to historical scan results for tracking progress
